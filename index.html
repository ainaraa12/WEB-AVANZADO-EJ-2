WRAITH STATION

Welcome to Wraith Station a horror video game developed by Strait Jacket Drip.

Into the underground

Become a metro guard.

You are now Alex Rivas, a seasoned security guard. After a series of unfortunate events, you've reached a point in your life where you feel at the lowest point in your live; the only thing you're looking for is something stable and hassle-free... You stumble upon a new gig: being a subway security guard.

Brave the legends.

The only thing you have to do is patrol each station, something easy peasy... However, you didn't count on the fact that its urban legends go beyond reality: paralyzing sounds, creepy creatures that whisper in your ear, spectral figures that stalk you; the most disturbing things in the city...

Will your sanity survive?
